Your privacy

Marvin is a well-behaved app and does not require a user account to operate. As such, Marvin does not transmit or disclose any user data with us with the following exceptions. To provide a better user experience and functionality, the app may be set up to use services from third parties. These services are described below and are subject to their own privacy policies.

Dropbox

In order to access your books easily you may link Marvin to your Dropbox account. This is entirely optional and set up by you.

  • Marvin uses Dropbox's APIs and infrastructure to handle logins and file transfers. As such, neither we nor the app have access to your Dropbox username and/or password, or your files.
  • Marvin scans the contents of your Dropbox to list files having EPUB, CBR, CBZ, and other relevant file types.
  • Files are only downloaded upon your explicit request and never automatically uploaded.
  • For performance reasons, Marvin caches the list of relevant file types (.epub, .cbz, ...). This cache is only stored on your device and is not transmitted to or shared with anyone (please see note on iCloud backups).
  • Dropbox have their own, independent privacy policy and we have no responsibility or liability for their activities.

Other third party links and content

When using Marvin you may optionally access websites and/or other services such as diagnostics services, online text translation tools, and online encyclopediae. These services have their own privacy policies and we have no responsibility or liability for their activities. Such services include:

  • Some eBooks may contain links to websites and other services.
  • While you are reading a book or performing other activities in Marvin you may perform lookups to websites and other services such as encyclopaedia and translation lookups.
  • Optionally, you may configure Marvin to use the services of other apps installed on your device (e.g. dictionary apps, posting to Facebook or Twitter).
  • If you include Marvin in your iCloud backup, all of its content will be backed up at Apple.
  • When you set up your device you may have enabled sending diagnostics usage to Apple. If you opted to share this information with "App Developers" some of this information may be made available to us to improve Marvin and troubleshoot problems. This setting may be turned off from the Settings app of your device.

Children

In order to enhance your reading experience, Marvin allows you to access external web content. As such the app is rated 17+ on the App Store. We never collect or maintain information from those we actually know are under 13, and no part of the app is designed or structured to attract anyone under 13.
